🌸 ‹𝟹♡☺ Visited art gallery ᯓ ᡣ𐭩

I am also interested in art. During these 3 years, I have visited many art galleries. Among them is Ilham Gallery, Puteh Gallery, Balai Seni Negara, Ur Mu Museum, G13 Gallery and The Goddown Arts Centre.

‧₊˚ ☁️⋅♡𓂃 ࣪ ִֶָ☾ all my favorite art galleries like 🖼️🎨

Ilham Gallery Ilham Gallery, situated at Level 5, ILHAM Tower, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, showcases contemporary art from Malaysia and Southeast Asia. Featuring diverse artworks, from paintings to multimedia installations, the gallery hosts dynamic exhibitions exploring modern themes. Open Tuesday to Sunday, it offers a vibrant cultural experience in the heart of the city.
National Art Gallery Balai Seni Negara, located in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, is the national art gallery featuring a vast collection of Malaysian art. It showcases traditional and contemporary works, including paintings, sculptures, and installations. Open daily, the gallery offers a rich cultural experience and insight into Malaysia's artistic heritage
Puteh Gallery Puteh Gallery, located in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, is a contemporary art space dedicated to promoting emerging and established artists. The gallery features diverse artworks, from paintings to sculptures, highlighting innovative and thought-provoking pieces. Open throughout the week, it offers visitors a dynamic and inspiring cultural experience.
Ur Mu Museum Ur Mu Gallery, located in Bukit Bintang, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, showcases contemporary art with a focus on innovative and experimental works. The gallery features diverse artworks, including paintings, sculptures, and installations. Open daily, it offers a dynamic cultural experience in the vibrant heart of Kuala Lumpur
